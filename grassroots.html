Grassroots

Grassroots boxing


Most boxers start from a very young age training how to box at their local gym and spend years in the shadows perfecting their technique in punching, blocking, body movement, foot movement and how they move around the ring along with the arduous fitness and diet regime that is part and parcel of being a boxer. Whilst training, sparring and perfecting their boxing style they begin fighting at a junior level before moving into the amateurs, then they might become professional fighters with some skipping the amateurs going straight into professional fighting. Obviously, the exeption being YouTube boxers who go from knowing very little about boxing to fighting in a ring with crowds watching at a huge venue with a pay-per-view audience within a very short space of time.
